day 9 (7/4/2024): the aftermath

the test was rather unusually easy

i was happy at first because i did the test very well, but it turned into great concern for quite an obvious reason

due to the test being easy, marks will skyrocket

this can have serious effects on my chance to win a prize

the olympic takes percentages of the number of participants to award them prizes, and so high marks will lead to high prizes standard

like usually you only need about 15/20 to get the second prize, but because of the "score inflation", a 17/20 may not even be enough for a third prize

this fact has troubled me a lot since the test day
